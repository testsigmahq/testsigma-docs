---

Use Copilot to reorder test steps by dragging and dropping them into the desired sequence. Copilot provides a drag-and-drop interface that lets you rearrange steps directly in the steps panel without leaving the session. This article discusses reordering test steps using Copilot.

---

> <p id="prerequisites">Prerequisites</p>
>
> Before you begin, ensure that:
>    - You have created a test case.
>    - You have installed and configured the **Testsigma Terminal**.
>    - You have referred to the documentation on [Launch Copilot](https://testsigma.com/docs/copilot/launch-copilot/).

---

## **Steps to Reorder Test Steps**

1. In the **Copilot** dialog, click on the step you want to move. The cursor changes to a grab icon indicating the step is draggable.
   ![Copilot](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/reorder_copilot_1.png)

2. Click and drag the step to the desired position in the list. The target position is highlighted as you drag.
   ![Drag and drop](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/reorder_copilot_2.png)

3. Click **Save Changes** to confirm the new order, or click **Discard** to revert to the original order.
   ![Save changes](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/reorder_copilot_3.png)

[[info | **NOTE**:]]
| - Drag-and-drop reordering is disabled during recording, execution, or when a step is being edited.
