While Testsigma Terminal extracts the required files, you may encounter issues due to insufficient disk space or other software blocking the extraction process. This article discusses how to resolve the issue. 

---

## **Steps to Resolve the Issue**

1. In the **Testsigma Terminal** window, click **Retry**. This restarts the extraction process.
   ![Retry](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_1.png) 

2. If the issue persists, click **Troubleshoot Download**. 
   ![Troubleshoot Download](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_2.png)

3. Click **Download ZIP** to download the installer ZIP files to your device. 
   ![Download ZIP](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_3.png)

4. After the ZIP file is downloaded, select **Continue**. 
   ![Continue](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_4.png)

5. Select **Select downloaded .zip file** and choose the ZIP file you downloaded.
   ![Download ZIP](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_5.png)

6. Click **Continue** to begin the extraction.
   ![Continue](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/extraction_failed_6.png)
