When the required ports on your system are already in use or blocked, the Testsigma Terminal may fail to start. This article discusses how to resolve the issue. 

---

## **Steps to Resolve the Issue**

1. In the **Testsigma Terminal** window, click **Retry**. This checks the portâ€™s availability again.
   ![Retry](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/ports_1.png)

2. If the issue persists, click **Edit Configuration**. 
   ![Edit Configuration](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/ports_2.png)

3. In the **Edit Configuration** dialog, check the available ports on your device, update them, and click **Update Configuration & Retry**.
   ![Edit Configuration](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/troubleshooting_for_copilot/ports_3.png)
