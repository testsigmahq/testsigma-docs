In Testsigma, you can upload small files as attachments and then use their links in your test steps. This document will discuss how to do that.

---
## **Steps to Upload Files**

1. Navigate to **Test Data > Uploads**.
   ![Uploads](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_1.png)

2. Click on **Upload**.
   ![Upload File](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_2.png)

3. On **Upload a File** prompt, drag and drop your files or click on **Browse** to select the file from your local folder. 
![Prompt](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_3.png)

4. Enter **Name**, **Version**, and click on **Upload**. 
![Details](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_4.png)

---

## **Updating an Uploaded File**

1. Navigate to **Test Data > Uploads**. 
![Uploads](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_1.png)

2. Click the ellipsis icon against the required upload and click **Upload new version**. 
![Kebab Menu](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_5.png)

3. In the **Update new version** dialog, drag and drop your files or click on **Browse** to select the file from your local folder. 
![Update File](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_6.png)

4. Enter **Name**, **Version**, and click on **Upload**.
![Details](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_7.png)

---

## **Getting the file URL**
From the Uploads page, copy the file path from the copy button and paste this URL when needed in the test steps. 
![Copy Path](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_upload_8.png)

*For more information about using uploaded files in Test Steps, refer to the [documentation on test steps for uploaded files](https://testsigma.com/tutorials/test-cases/web-apps/automate-upload-buttons/)*
